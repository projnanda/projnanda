## What fellows build
- Protocols and reference implementations for decentralized agents
- Interoperability across MCP, A2A, and emerging runtimes
- Governance, trust, and attestation flows for safe coordination
- SDKs, demos, and capstone projects that ship to the community

## Who should apply
- Strong Python, Go, or Rust skills with distributed systems experience
- Open-source track record and bias to build
- Interest in privacy, orchestration, and secure agent ecosystems
